About Armenia

 Armenia is one of the earliest cradles of human civilization. It is a great destination for those who are eager to look into ancient times or the roots of Christianity, as in 301 AD Armenia adopted Christianity, becoming the first country in the world to proclaim it as a state religion.
 Armenia is an open air museum. There are a lot of places of interest here. Being a site of ancient civilization, it has a lot of historical monuments all over its territory. Besides, it is one of the safest countries in the world and people are very friendly and hospitable.
